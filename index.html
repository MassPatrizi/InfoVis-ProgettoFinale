Networks Graph for Top 100 BoardGames from BoardGameGeek.com

Interact with the graph by hovering the cursor over a node or by clicking on it.
Hovering over a node lets you visualize the kind of connections that game has with other games,

Links color legend:
A red link means that players of that game liked the other one, and vice versa [strongest connection,
this is what the graph shows by default without needing to interact with it.]

A green link means that the players of the selected game liked the other one.

A blue link means that the game is liked by the other game's players.

Click on a game to show a popup containing all the info about that game:

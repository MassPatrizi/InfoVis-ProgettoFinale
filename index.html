Networks Graph for Top 100 BoardGames from BoardGameGeek.com

Here are some instructions on how to navigate the graph:

Hove over a game-node to show a brief description of that game and the type of relation that he has with other games:

An orange link means that players of that game liked the other one, and viceversa.
A blue link means that only the players of the selected game liked the other one.
A green link means that the game is liked by the other game's players.

Click on a game to show a popup containing all the info about that game
